Consumer Sales Forecasting

In the realm of predictive analytics, my Kaggle project, "Consumer Sales Forecasting Model," addresses a pivotal challenge faced by a Ecommerce company. Utilizing feature engineering, I seamlessly integrated these into the dataset, enhancing its depth. The pivotal phase included constructing a robust sentiment discerning machine learning model.
